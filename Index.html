Hospital Management Software
is a computer system that helps manage information related to health care and aids in effective the job completion for health care providers.Management of data related to all departments within the healthcare facility, such as; Clinical, Financial, and Laboratory.



Digital Payments
Is the use of Visa, MasterCard, Mobile Money & Insurance to pay for health services and prescriptions by clients.



Telemedicines
It is the use of technology that enables remote healthcare (telehealth). Basically it makes it possible for physicians to treat patients whenever needed and wherever the patient is, by using a computer or smartphone.



Diagnostics
It is using technology or equipment such as MRS Scan, CT Scan, Mammo Graphy, Ultra Sound, E.C.G and X-Ray.



Logistics Management System
It is an Inventory Management, Procurement and Supply of Medicines and consumables.
